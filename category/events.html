1st Mini-workshop PREMs

Published: mer. 18 juin 2025
By Hideo Aochi

In Events.

Mini-workshop is goind to be held at ENS Paris and on-line on the Thursday 10th July 2025.

8h55 Short introduction

9h00 (16h00 in Tokyo) - Scientific presentation (In English, Hybrid)

Speakers: Tentative titles

9h00 Blandine Gardonio (ISTerre, University of Savoie Mont Blanc) : The use of template matching technique in continental and oceanic subductions.

9h25 Weifan Lu (Univ. Beijing, zoom) : Tidal modulation of seismicity in Southern California and Japan.

9h50 Samson Marty (Caltech): An analytical model for elastic stresses induced by hydraulic fracturing: application to the Midland Basin

10h15 Ryoko Nakata (Univ. Tokyo, zoom) : Triggering of large earthquakes using multiscale circular patch model in quasi-dynamic numerical simulation of earthquake cycle

(10h40-10h45)

10h45 David Marsan (ISTerre, University of Savoie Mont Blanc) : Thermally activated static friction can explain earthquake interactions

11h10 Takahiro Hatano (Osaka Univ., zoom) : Triggering of unstable slip in granular matter

11h35 Francois Petrelis (LP-ENS) : Random geometry models and earthquake statistical properties

12h00 (19h in Tokyo) - Lunch or Dinner
